# Privacy Policy
This app ("Chinese") respects and protects user privacy. Our privacy policy is as follows:
1. This app only obtains the user's Pi account authorization information via the Pi SDK, which is used for in-app identity verification and Pi coin transactions. No other personal information of the user will be collected or stored.
2. We will not share or sell the user's Pi account information to any third party.
3. If the user uninstalls this app, the authorization information associated with the Pi SDK will automatically become invalid.
